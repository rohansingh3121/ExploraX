Coronavirus India Live Updates: Goa govt to rope in AYUSH doctors to treat Covid-19 patients

October 9, 2020

Goa Chief Minister Pramod Sawant on Friday said that starting next week the state government will rope in AYUSH doctors in providing treatment to COVID-19 patients with mild to moderate symptoms, news agency PTI reported.

“From next week, the government will involve AYUSH doctors in treating COVID-19 patients with mild to moderate symptoms, along with other government doctors,” Sawant said. “They would also be involved in post-COVID management of patients in the state,” he added.

Meanwhile, India continues to record a massive surge in its Covid tally with as many as 70,000 cases in a single day. While the overall caseload stood at 6,906,151, the number of fatalities rose to 1,06,490.

Globally, over 36.4 million people have been infected while more than 1.05 million have succumbed to the virus. The United States continues to remain the worst-affected followed by India and Brazil.
