After Baba ka Dhaba, Kerala woman receives help for her dhaba

October 13, 2020

Richa Chadha has shared the story of an old lady running a dhaba in Kerala, who is currently struggling to meet ends. Despite being the second-most popular dhaba in Kerala, she is facing lack of customers, which is making survival during COVID-19 difficult for her.

Richa shared a video in which the lady, Parvathyamma, is talking about her situation, and despite it all, smiling. She received help from Keralites and is currently able to sustain for a while.

"Kerala Story : This old lady runs a Dhaba in order to feed her family. She doesn't have customers & struggles to earn. It’s resilient and delicious Parvathyamma’s eatery at Karimba, near Mannarkkad. After Baba ka Dhaba, Keralites turn to help this elderly woman. #BabaKaDhaba," a user shared alongside the video.

Richa quote tweetd the video and wrote, "Show up for Parvathyamma! Come on. at Karimba!"

The heartwarming story comes to light after a 'Baba' making vadas in Agra received help from a blogger, who spread the word and helped increase his customers. #BabaKaOnlineDhaba also started thanks to the initiative.
